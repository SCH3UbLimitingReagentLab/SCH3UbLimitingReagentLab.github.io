Objective

The objective of this experiment is to understand the impact of a limiting reagent on a chemical reaction by determining the limiting reagent in a reaction of vinegar and baking soda. To conduct this experiment, vinegar was poured into an empty water bottle and baking soda was poured into a balloon. The balloon was wrapped around the neck of the bottle, sealing the bottle and the baking soda in the balloon was poured into the vinegar. This trial was repeated one more time in which the quantity of baking soda was decreased.
